# ARCZEN UNIFIED PLATFORM — COMPLETE FEATURE GUIDE

This document details the features of the consolidated ArcZen platform, including the Public Client Account and the internal CuteAdmin Panel.

---

## 🏬 PUBLIC CLIENT ACCOUNT (Custoemr Area)
Located at: https://arczen.store/account/

### 1. Secure Authentication
*   **Registration**: Streamlined account creation for new customers.
*   **Google & Email Login**: Support for multiple login methods for ease of access.
*   **Password Recovery**: Automated system for resetting forgotten passwords via email.

### 2. Customer Profile
*   **Personal Info**: Management of full name, email address, and avatar.
*   **Preferences**: Storage of user-specific site settings and interaction history.

### 3. Order & Digital Goods Management
*   **Transaction History**: A comprehensive log of all past purchases.
*   **Digital Delivery System**: Instant access to purchased gaming items, subscriptions, and digital credentials.
*   **Order Tracking**: Real-time status updates for ongoing and fulfilled orders.

---

## 🦄 CUTEADMIN PANEL (Internal Management System)
Located at: http://localhost:4321/cuteadmin/ (Internal Unified Path)

### 1. Unified Dashboard Center
*   **KPI Visualization**: High-level metrics for Total Tasks, Completion Rates, and Overdue Items.
*   **Live Activity Feed**: Real-time stream of system-wide actions and updates.
*   **Recent Task List**: Quick-access view of local and assigned work.

### 2. Task & Workflow Management
*   **Advanced Task Engine**: Create, update, and prioritize tasks with status tracking.
*   **Team Assignment**: Direct assignment of projects to specific team members.
*   **Resource Management**: Linking tasks to external documents and project files.

### 3. Attendance & Performance Tracker
*   **Daily Log System**: Automated tracking of entry and exit times for employees.
*   **Performance Metrics**: Calculation of total productive hours and consistency over time.

### 4. Points & Rewards (Gamification)
*   **Achievement System**: Assigning reward points for completed projects and meeting deadlines.
*   **Leaderboard**: Tracking top-performing employees within the team.

### 5. Team & Authorization Suite
*   **Zero-Trust Whitelist**: Only authorized emails can access the admin panel.
*   **Role-Based Access (RBAC)**: Fine-grained permissions for Admin, Moderator, and Employee.
*   **Masquerade Mode**: Admin capability to view the panel from other user roles for support and testing.

### 6. Store Operations
*   **Product Manager**: Inventory management for digital goods, including pricing and stock levels.
*   **Order Fulfillment Center**: Centralized area for managing customer purchases and ensuring delivery.
*   **Customer CRM**: Comprehensive database of all store customers and their lifetime engagement.
*   **Sales Analytics**: Graphical representation of store performance and sales trends.

### 7. Internal Management System (IMS)
*   **IMS Client CRM**: B2B database for agency-level creative services.
*   **IMS Project Tracking**: Lifecycle management for high-value client projects.
*   **Professional Invoicing**: Tool for generating and managing professional billing for clients.

### 8. System Governance & Security
*   **Activity Log / System Audit**: Complete visibility into sensitive platform actions, security events, and data changes.
*   **Touch of the Founder**: Exclusive, top-level control panel for the platform owner.
*   **Company Settings**: Global configuration for branding, system defaults, and platform parameters.
*   **Content CMS Link**: Integrated access point for managing site-wide markdown content (Products, Portfolio, Services).
